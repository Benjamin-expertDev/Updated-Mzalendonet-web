We offer reliable high-speed Internet services in Nairobi, Kiambu, Machakos, and Kajiado counties. Our Internet options include both Fiber and Wireless connections. In Fiber-ready zones, we install cables from our poles to your location to set up a fiber router. In areas without fiber readiness, we provide stable wireless connectivity with a one-time installation fee.

In the event that the router's "LOS" (Loss Of Signal) area is consistently blinking red, contact customer care right away. It essentially means there is no connection between your router and our Internet servers.
Forgetting your WiFi login password is common. You can always reach out to our customer support team for assistance.

We offer the most affordable, reliable and Dependable Internet. Pocket Friendly.

We guarantee installation for our customers within 24 hours. Thus, depending on the backlog, the installation time may range from one hour to twenty-four hours. Although we always consult with the client and choose a time that is convenient for both parties if the timeframe changes.

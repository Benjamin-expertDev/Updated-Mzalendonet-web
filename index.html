Fiber cables are available for our Internet services. Thus, fiber is installed in the residence. To connect a fiber router to you, we first facilitate running cables from our poles to your location.

We guarantee installation for our customers within 24 hours. Thus, depending on the backlog, the installation time may range from one hour to twenty-four hours. Although we always consult with the client and choose a time that is convenient for both parties if the timeframe changes.

In the event that the router's "LOS" area is consistently blinking red, contact customer care right away. Your account has not expired or is not due for renewal only because the indicator is blinking red. It essentially means there is no connection between your router and our Internet servers. This can be the result of Fiber Cut, among other things.
